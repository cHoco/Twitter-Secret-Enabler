Twitter Secret Enabler by cHoco

A simple application that aims to enable the secret panel in Twitter for Mac preferences.

Recently I had to reinstall Snow Leopard after ther 10.6.8 update, and for some reason, the Finder got corrupted during the installation, and I couldn't navigate my filesystem anymore. So I had to completely erase my hard-drive and reinstall everything. But during the restore process of my backup the applications' preferences got lost. The most annoying thing was that Twitter didn't have the secret panel enabled when I reinstalled it and I couldn't enter my macheist credentials anymore. But fortunately the algorithm behind the license check is pretty simple, so with some reverse engineering I created this little app. Hope you all enjoy :D
